Glitch Art

"Distance between us"

This series was created using Audacity and Notepad++. It required meticulous editing, cutting and pasting, and effects to glitch the original image.

Identity, love, and connection are the themes of these glitch art pieces.

×
×
×

The first glitch resembles the difficulties of self-expression. In today's society, it seems unacceptable to dress or express our natural selves. With this glitch, it expresses the way society tries to block us from being ourselves.

The second glitch is the embodiment of being in a relationship. Being with your significant other is about closessness and bond. Sharing that bond and connection makes our personalities and personas become one.

The third glitch is the feeling of love. The coloring of this glitch was originally black and white but with the editing, it caused heat signature colors to be exposed. This vision allows us to view how love spreads.
