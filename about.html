About Me

Hi, I’m Sabrina Hung, a digital artist and creative enthusiast. I specialize in various forms of visual expression, from glitch art to 3D modeling and net art. My passion for digital media allows me to explore new creative boundaries and push the limits of imagination. I’m always eager to learn new techniques and challenge myself with exciting projects.

My Creative Process

My work is driven by experimentation and a desire to blend technology with artistic expression. I enjoy working with various tools like Audacity, Blender, and Photoshop to create art that resonates on both aesthetic and conceptual levels. Each project is a new opportunity for me to grow as an artist and expand my skills.
