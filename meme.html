Meme Mashup

Not a chill guy anymore...This is a meme mashup of "Chill guy", also known as "My new character" by Phillip Banks (2023) & "Arthur's Fist", also known as "Clenched Fist" (2016). I used Adobe Photoshop to seamlessly combine the hand with the character. Adding a change to the character's facial expression caused the meaning to change as well. He started a Chill Guy but has decided to let go.
